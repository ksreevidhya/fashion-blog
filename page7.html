Impact: What I Learned About Craft, Artisans, and Luxury

A. Cultural Impact

He revived craft traditions like:

  • Benarasi weaving
  • resham aari work
  • Rajasthani gota
  • Bengal’s khadi and Kantha

This helped me recognise how designers influence cultural preservation.

B. Economic Impact

Working with 3000+ artisans, he reinforces the value of handcrafted labour. Learning this made me more aware of ethical fashion and responsible consumption.

C. Fashion Industry Impact

He shifted Indian couture toward:

  • slow fashion
  • heritage revival
  • artisanal luxury

This made me rethink how the future of Indian fashion is deeply tied to craft—not just commercial trends.
